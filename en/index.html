Why Sociocracy?

Our ways of working together are often outdated. Many organizations remain stuck in rigid hierarchical structures that stifle creativity, engagement, and initiative. People feel lost, decisions are slow, and innovation suffers.

Sociocracy, born in the Netherlands and used worldwide, is a social technology offering proven practices for more effective and inclusive collaboration: shared power, everyone's voice heard.

With Sociocracy 3.0, the modern and agile version, we help organizations unlock everyone's potential to innovate together and adapt quickly to change.

Governance plumber, sociocracy consultant

My background: Researcher in decision-making under uncertainty, I studied how complex systems adapt to change. Several years as a data scientist in tech companies, where I learned that the best data means little without good collective decisions.
